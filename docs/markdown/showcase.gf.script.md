# Showcase 202603

## GF Showcase Script

Hi everyone, I am Charly from the Good Food engineering team.

I will give a quick update on what we shipped and what is next.

### Slide 1 - Universal Linking

Let me start with universal linking.

On mobile, readers can now open Good Food content directly in the app from a browser or another app.

So instead of bouncing web pages, they land straight in the app and continue reading. Less friction, and a cleaner journey.

Since January, we have seen around 60,000 referred opens through universal linking,
so we know this path is already being used at scale.

### Slide 2 - Topic Pages

Next is topic pages.

We now have more destination pages that are easy to reach from the Good Food app main pages.

A topic page can be shaped around a category, a topic, or an asset type, depending on what we want to list.

This gives the app better ways to guide users to relevant content. Readers find the articles faster, and discovery feels less noisy.

### Slide 3 - Commercial

Now on to the commercial - The Lexus campaign.

We partnered on a Lexus sponsored content unit which is "The Road Trip", that supports a brand campaign without breaking the homepage experience.

The team delivered curated collections that feel native to Good Food while still meeting more commercial objectives.

This was a cross-functional effort between Design, Engineering, and Ad Tech, and we shipped the feature very smoothly because every team was aligned from the start.

This is bigger than one campaign.
We now have a practical model the Commercial team can reuse for future integrations across the products.

### Slide 4 - What's Next

So what is next?

Gifting articles for AFR apps; this is an active focus now.

And Vertex AI integration.
We are working to bring this to users soon, with search results that are more context-aware and useful.

Finally, map improvements.
We want users to find places they care about/ with higher quality results,
We are going to explore multiple perspectives to make that happen.

That is the update from the Good Food team.

Thanks everyone. I will now hand over to AFR.
